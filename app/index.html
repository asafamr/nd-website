What is NDJS?

NDJS is open source framework that helps you create beautiful installers using latest web technologies

Features

User experience
Cool things come in cool packages -
make a good first impression Use web styling capabilities for responsive, engaging UI that maintain your product look and feel
Quality
Installers are on your critical path -
ensure quality NDJS allows full testing and debugging capabilities for your code. Use modren IDEs and scripting languages, well known and tested frameworks and packages from NPM and Bower
Open source
Full configurability
NDJS is completely open source allowing you to tweak and configure it to your own needs
